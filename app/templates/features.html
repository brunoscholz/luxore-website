App Features

Looks

Looks are our fuel. The value of the network is here! We want to incentivize creative people to become powerful.

Vitrine and Search

Every person or business has a vitrine; This is your showcase. The search is your way to find exactly what you want in the best way possible.

Business

You can create business pages that can be, pretty much, whatever you want: ecommerce, studios, blogs, teaching classes, etc.

LUX coin

A coin based on the same principles of Lisk and Bitcoin and is the main incentive in the network.

Gamification

Through tecniques inherited of games, this is another incentive factor of the network; Things like competition, achievements and scores are here!

News Feed

Besides important announcements of the network itself, you can sign to your favorite blogs and channels to stay always updated.
