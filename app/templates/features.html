Looks

The looks, your photos, will live in your vitrine - a fancy french name for the "feed". Your followers see everything you post there, following a style, a trend, or your creativity.. You can explore your looks, your body type, events, make-up, hair-style, shoes, underwear, socks, bracelets, necklaces... anything you want. To describe the look you can tag it, like "party formal dinner long-dress", plus situations such as wedding, new years eve, or walking my dog on the beach, and body types like pear, plus-size, skinny, etc. The idea is to be found! When you apply tags to you looks, people can find you and cheer your work if they like it. It's in the looks page that you'll find the ads.

Coin

Yeah, we have a coin! LUX is a token, a cryto-currency, built on top of Lisk which is a platform for building blockchains and decentralized apps!
The idea is to cut off the bureocracy and, like other crypto-currencies, be safe. All the financial transactions inside Luxore network will be made with LUX. That guarantees the valuation of the whole system. There will be created (according to this chart) 1 billion coins in two years, and it will not be possible to create more or less than this! Every user will have an address that works like your bank account number and you can send and receive money with it, protect by a passphrase that only you know. (read more).

Business

Here you can make business following your wishes, talents and inspirations. You can sell fashion consulting, create studios with other professionals, tell about your day, make a tutorial, or even sell your products. You can generate revenue in any way other people voluntary agree. These spaces put people in contact, linking customer and supplier, master and padawan, buyer and seller. Business spaces have a vitrine too, so they can be like any other user and post looks, lists and products.
