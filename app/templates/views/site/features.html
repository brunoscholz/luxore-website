Luxore is a new kind of social network where you make your looks, share them, become a reference and inspire your followers.

Looks

The Looks, your photos, will live in your Vitrine - a fancy french name for the "feed". Your followers see everything you post there, following a style, a trend, or your creativity... You can explore your looks, your body type, events, make-up, hair-style, shoes, underwear, socks, bracelets, necklaces... Anything you want. The idea is to be found and be on command of your own style!

Tags and Search

To describe the look you can tag it, like "party formal dinner long-dress", plus situations such as wedding, new years eve, or walking my dog on the beach, and body types like pear, plus-size, skinny, etc. When you apply tags to your looks, people can find you and cheer your work. Tags make it easy to find everything about fashion filtering the search results by what matters to you!

Profile

Your profile is your virtual identity! It shows your info in an organized way with tools for customization and security settings... Here you can choose your interests which will filter your Vitrine showing always what is relevant to you. Every info about you will live here and you can control it the way you want - your badges, your data, your friends, rankings, lists, galleries, stats, etc..

Coin

Yeah, we have a coin! LUX is a token, a cryto-currency, built on top of Lisk which is a platform for building blockchains and decentralized apps!
The idea is to cut off the bureocracy and, like other crypto-currencies, be safe. All the financial transactions inside Luxore network will be made with LUX. That guarantees the valuation of the whole system. There will be created 1 billion coins in two years, and it will not be possible to create more or less than this! Every user will have an address that works like your bank account number and you can send and receive money with it, protected by a passphrase that only you know.

Studios

Here you can make business following your wishes, talents and inspirations. You can sell fashion consulting, create studios with other professionals, tell about your day, make a tutorial, or even sell your products. You can generate revenue in any way other people voluntary agree. These virtual coworking put people in contact, linking customer and supplier, master and padawan, buyer and seller. Studios have a vitrine too, like any other user, and you can post looks, lists and products.

News

O Jornal é um espaço onde você mesmo poderá criar o conteúdo. Aqui você terá espaço para anunciar suas marcas, produtos e serviços. Sua oportunidade de fazer negócios e ficar sempre atualizado com tudo que rola no mundo da moda. Você também encontrará os cases e pessoas que mais se destacaram na rede com reportagens que contam o que estas pessoas estão fazendo para seu destaque.

Gamification

A feature borrowed from games are achievements systems. They stimulate and engage people. Thophies and badges are virtual rewards given to users by their performance and they will be tied to professional paths: if you like make-up, you can post about it and become a reference by going deeper into the subject, gaining visibility, having others loving your work, and for each accomplishment you will have a badge, a title, or a trophy to show and proof of your dedication! They can be used as guide showing the way to more engagement and earnings. In games, characters with a higher level are more likely to beat the challenges of the stages, users with higher achievements beat easily the barriers of visualization... But all in funny way!

Social

Being social is the whole point so don't be mean - read about our ethical guidelines here. When you like a look, you should show your apreciation as well as when you dislike it. To any look, you have 2 buttons: cheer and boo! They are the more common way you can interact with others and participate in the network... You can also share the look with your followers or put it inside a list... Of course, you can leave a comment too. The comments can be deleted by the Look owner or they can be flaged as inappropriated: a snake will be placed in it so other people know that is a mean comment... Users can block other users for whatever reason they see fit!

Tips

“Every day you encounter people or causes that inspire you to help, to applaud, to support.” (https://www.changetip.com/). Indivíduos criativos inspiram outros! Integramos às funcionalidades o TIP; usuários podem mostrar sua apreciação e suporte através de gorjetas diretamente para quem o inspira. É algo superior ao simples “gostei”! Isso gera um estímulo ao aumento da qualidade das fotos.. Quem não quer ser reconhecido?! As pessoas poderão apoiar os looks com uma espécie de gorjetas. Por liberalidade, o usuário pode usufruir das tips para motivar quem postou o look.

Lists

Everyone likes lists! They are a handy way of organizing content and they are full of meaning. You can made a list of the best looks of the last decade, for example. It will enable looks to live more. Funny and expressive they can be shared and also boost your visibility or business. You can have a wishlist, for example, so people can know how to gift you in your birthday!!

Notifications

Notifications keep you informed of what is going on with your posts, your businesses, and your friends! It's a way that we can communicate and show you possibilities like advertisers willing to pay you for your services. This can be used as a way to discover the world beyond who you are following... You can receive notifications from studios, from people related to your interests, or even tags!

Messenger

Direct channel of instant communication between users! Like the rest of the network, all messages will be encrypted.
