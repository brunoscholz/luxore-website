Luxore is built to keep the focus on fashion. All features have something to contribute to that!

Looks

The Looks, your photos, will live in your Vitrine - a fancy french name for the "feed". Your followers will see everything you post there, following a style, a trend, or your creativity... You can explore your looks, your body type, events, make-up, hair-style, shoes, underwear, socks, bracelets, necklaces... Anything you want. The idea is to be found and be on command of your own style!

Tags and Search

To describe your looks you can tag it, like "party formal dinner long-dress", plus situations such as wedding, new years eve, or walking the dog on the beach, and body types like pear, plus-size, skinny, etc. When you apply tags to your looks, people can find you and cheer your work. Tags make it easy to find everything about fashion by filtering the search results and showing only what matters to you!

Profile

Your profile is your virtual identity! It shows your info in an organized way with tools for customization and security settings... Here you can choose your interests which will filter your Vitrine showing always what is relevant to you. All info about you will live here and you can control it the way you want - your badges, your data, your friends, rankings, lists, galleries, stats, etc..

Coin

Yeah, we have a coin! LUX is a token, a crytocurrency, built with the same premises of Bitcoin using Lisk: a platform for building blockchains and decentralized apps!
The idea is to cut off the bureaucracy and, like other cryptocurrencies, be safe. All the financial transactions inside Luxore network will be made with LUX. That guarantees the valuation of the whole system. There will be created 1 billion LUX Coins in two years, and it will not be possible to create more or less than this! Every user will have an address that works like your bank account number and you can send and receive money with it, protected by a passphrase that only you know.

Studios

Here you can make business following your wishes, talents and inspirations. You can sell fashion consulting, create studios with other professionals, tell about your day, make a tutorial, or even sell your products. You can generate revenue in any way you want and other people voluntarily agree. These virtual co-working put people in contact, linking together customer and supplier, teacher and apprentice, buyer and seller. Studios have a vitrine too, like any other user, and you can post looks, lists and products.

News

It is a place to put together internal and external content. It will work like the rss readers where you subscribe to a blog to receive its posts. Besides that, it will have a yellow pages section where you can announce brands, products and services... Through the Luxore blog you'll always count with interviews and curiosities about the network and the world of fashion around us!

Gamification

A feature borrowed from games are achievements systems. They stimulate and engage people. Thophies and badges are virtual rewards that are given to users by their performance and they will be tied to professional paths: if you like make-up, you can post about it and become a reference by going deeper into the subject, gaining visibility, having others loving your work, and for each accomplishment you will have a badge, a title, or a trophy to show as proof of your dedication! They can be used as a guide showing the way and the possibilities inside the network, leading to more earnings. In games, characters with a higher level are more likely to beat the challenges of the stages, users with higher achievements beat easily the barriers of visualization... But all in a funny way!

Social

Being social is the whole point so don't be mean - read about our ethical guidelines. When you like a look, you should show your apreciation as well as when you dislike it. To any look, you have 2 buttons: cheer and boo! This is the more common way you can interact with others and participate in the network... You can also share the look with your followers or put it inside a list... Of course, you can leave a comment too. The comments can be deleted by the Look owner or they can be flaged as inappropriated: a snake (or something) will be placed in it so other people know that it is a mean comment... Users can block other users for whatever reason they see fit!

Tips

"Every day you encounter people or causes that inspire you to help, to applaud, to support." ChangeTip started this brilliant project, and we are going to do the same, in LUX inside Luxore. Creative individuals inspire everyone around them and they ususaly can use some support. With the tips feature you can show your appreciation and support in a way beyond likes and sharing! Directly to who inspires you...

Lists

Everyone likes lists! They are a handy way of organizing content and they are full of meaning. You can make a list of the best looks of the last decade, for example. It will enable looks to live more. Funny and expressive they can be shared and also boost your visibility or your business. You can have a wishlist, for example, so people can know what to give you in your birthday!!

Notifications

Notifications keep you informed of what is going on with your posts, your businesses, and your friends! It's a way that we can communicate and show you possibilities like advertisers willing to pay you by your services. This can be used as a way to discover the world beyond who you are following... You can receive notifications from studios, from people related to your interests, or even from tags!

Messenger

Direct channel of instant communication between users! Through this feature you can keep contact with friends and clients quickly and privately! Like the rest of the network, all messages will be encrypted so you can be sure this is a secure line of communication!
