LUX Coin

  • Decentralized

    Luxore will be running on top of Lisk, a platform for decentralized apps with their own sidechain. The network and the data will be spread in all users' phones and computers.

  • Secure

    Cryptocurrencies are secured by its decentralization and encryption. The transactions and data will be public, but encrypted and everywhere!

  • No Masters

    There will be only LUX 1 billion ever! No one can rule the creation or transactions of the coin. This means no one can interfere in it's value or form.

  • Valuable

    Value is subjective! So LUX value will be based on peoples will of using it. Since, inside Luxore, that is the only way of make transactions, it's value will be determined by market rules of supply and demand: sellers can ask the price they want, and buyers can pay what they want! All voluntary and peaceful.
