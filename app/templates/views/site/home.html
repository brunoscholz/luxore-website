LUX Coin

Our own coin, used to negotiate and reduce bureaucracy...

  • Decentralized

    Luxore will be running on top of Lisk, a platform for decentralized apps with their own sidechain. The network and the data will be spread in all users' devices.

  • Secure

    Cryptocurrencies are safe by its decentralization and encryption. The transactions and data will be public, but encrypted and everywhere! Everyone can verify the data, but no one can "see" it!

  • No Masters

    There will be only LUX 1 billion ever! No one can rule the creation or the transactions of the coin. This means no one can interfere in it's value or it's form. The coin is free from rulers, so it can be used by free people!

  • Valuable

    Value is subjective! So LUX value will be based on peoples will of using it. Since, inside Luxore, that is the only way to make transactions, it's value will be determined by market rules of supply and demand: sellers can ask the price they want, and buyers can pay what they want! All in a voluntary and peaceful way.

  1. Project was Born

    November 23th, 2015

    In a day of bad mood, Loris put some thought in how to make people's lives better... Fashion is all around us and can lift our moods, our self-estim... When she came with the idea, we started working on a solution: A social network with freedom, that could join people with the same interests - Luxore was born!! Since then, we are working hard to assemble it.

We are building Luxore to people. It's only fair they can be part of it since the begining.

  4. Kickstarter

    June 17th, 2016

    Although we are already building Luxore, we don't have all the resources we want... That's why we are launching the project on kickstarter - a platform for crowdfunding. In 30 days, we expect to raise the money so we can keep our schedule!

  5. Experiments

    December 1st, 2016

    From now on, we need a few early adopters to help us test everything... Find bugs, criticize the layouts, button positioning, colors, anything that could be a threat to usability or bad in the eye!
