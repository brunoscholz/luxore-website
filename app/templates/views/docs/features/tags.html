Creating

That's interesting!

Why?
  • It's one way to keep the network's focus on fashion
How
  • You need a badge of creator
  • You find that one tag is missing
  • You invest in LUX; Pay for the creation
And then?
  • The new tag will be validated and translated by other users with the money you paid
  • After that, everytime someone uses your tag, you'll receive a part of the revenue generate by the look using it

Verifying

The validation process is simple...

  • People can choose to "work" validating tags.
  • In every validation needed, a number of different users will be selected for this job
  • They will get paid by the initial investments of the creators
  • The creators will then have a tag and profit from it
